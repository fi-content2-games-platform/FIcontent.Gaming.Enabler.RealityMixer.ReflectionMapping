Detailed Description

Augmented Reality - Reflection Mapping enabler It provides a new level of realistic augmented reality rendering with the following steps: it pinpoints the center and radius of the material sphere source relative to the augmented reality marker. Then it assigns the camera image of the sphere to the source texturing of the target object(s) and performs spherical environment reflection mapping to the shading of the target object(s).
